Given, 
\begin{align}
\vec {A}&=\myvec {-2\\4\\-5}, \vec {B}=\myvec {1\\2\\3},
\\
\vec{B}-\vec{A}    &=\myvec{1\\2\\3} - \myvec{-2\\4\\-5}
     =\myvec{3\\-2\\8}
     \\
     \implies 
\norm{\vec{A}-\vec{B}} &=\sqrt{77}
    \end{align}
The unit vector is then calculated as
\begin{align}
\frac{\vec{A}-\vec{B}}{\norm{\vec{A}-\vec{B}}}
    =\frac{1}{\sqrt{77}}\myvec{3\\-2\\8}
\end{align}
