
The area of the required parallelogram is defined as
\begin{align}
\vec{a}\times \vec{b} =
\myvec{
0 & -3 & -1\\
3 & 0 & -1\\
1 & 1 & 0}
\myvec{
2\\
-7\\
1}
=\myvec{
20\\
5\\
-5}
\end{align}
Thus, the desired area is
\begin{align}
\norm{\vec{a}\times \vec{b}} &= \sqrt{(20)^{2}+(5)^{2}+(-5)^{2}} \\
&= 15\sqrt{2}
\end{align}
