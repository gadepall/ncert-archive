Let $\vec{U}$ be the unit vector in the direction of given vector and 
\begin{align}
    \vec{V} &= \myvec{2\\-1\\-2}
\end{align}    
Then
\begin{align}
 \norm{\vec{V}} &= \sqrt{(2)^2 + (-1)^2 + (-2)^2}\\
\implies  \norm{\vec{V}} &= 3
\end{align}
and 
\begin{align}
    \vec{U} &= \dfrac{\vec{V}}{\norm{\vec{V}}} = \frac{1}{3} \myvec{2\\-1\\-2}
\end{align}
